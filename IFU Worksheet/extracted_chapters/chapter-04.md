# Chapter 4: 赦罪的確據







> ** 詩 Psm 38:18**
> 我要承認我的罪孽 , 我要因我的罪憂愁。
> *I confess my iniquity; I am sorry for my sin.*

箴 Prov 28:13

遮掩自己罪過的 , 必不亨通。 承認離棄罪過的 , 必蒙憐恤。

> **No one who conceals transgressions will prosper, but one who confesses and forsakes them will obtain mercy. 羅 Rom 7:18-19**
> 我也知道在我裡頭 , 就是我肉體之中 , 沒有良善。 因為立志為善由得我 , 只是行出來由不得我。 故此 , 我所願意的善 , 我反不作。 我所不願意的惡 , 我倒去作。
> *For I know that nothing good dwells within me, that is, in my flesh. I can will what is right, but I cannot do it.*

弗 Eph 2:8-9

你們得救是本乎恩、也因著信 , 這並不是出於自己 , 乃是神所賜的 , 也不是出於行為 , 免得有人自誇。

> **For by grace you have been saved through faith, and this is not your own doing; it is the gift of God – not the result of works, so that no one may boast. 弗 Eph 4:32**
> 並要以恩慈相待 , 存憐憫的心 , 彼此饒恕 , 正如神在基督裡饒恕了你們一樣。
> *and be kind to one another, tenderhearted, forgiving one another, as God in Christ has forgiven you.*

約壹 1Joh 1:8-10

我們若說自己無罪,便是自欺,真理不在我們心裡了。 我們若認自己的罪,神是信實的、是公義的,必要赦免我們的罪,洗淨我們一切的不義。 我們若說自己沒有犯過罪,便是以神為說謊的,他的道也不在我們心裡了。

If we say that we have no sin, we deceive ourselves, and the truth is not in us. If we confess our sins, he who is faithful and just will forgive us our sins and cleanse us from all unrighteousness.If we say that we have not sinned, we make him a liar, and his word is not in us.
